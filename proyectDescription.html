Marvel Universe

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for exploring Marvel characters, creators, and their attributes like powers, origins, and affiliations. Users can search for characters and learn more about their backstories and abilities.

User Scenarios

  • Who are the authors and creators behind my favorite Marvel heroes?
  • What are the powers and abilities of Spider-Man?

Color Palette

Red (Primary Color): #E62429 - Used for titles, headings, call-to-action buttons, and highlighted links. Red is an iconic Marvel color, widely used in logos and branding.

Black (Secondary Color): #1A1A1A - Used for the main background and text. Black is frequently used in Marvel to create strong contrasts and convey a serious tone.

Gold/Yellow: #F4C542 - Used for accents, buttons, icons, and minor decorations to add emphasis and bring energy to the design.

Typography

Main Font: Roboto - Used for the body text and paragraphs, as it is legible and modern.

Secondary Font: Oswald - Used for titles and subtitles, providing a bold, comic-like style, perfect for the Marvel theme.

For buttons and highlighted elements, a bold or uppercase font style can be used to make them stand out with more energy.
